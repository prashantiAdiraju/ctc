    ACR Accreditation

    Since 1987, the ACR has accredited more than 36,000 facilities in 10 imaging modalities. The ACR offers accreditation programs in CT, MRI, breast MRI, nuclear medicine and PET as mandated under the Medicare Improvements for Patients and Providers Act (MIPPA) as well as for modalities mandated under the Mammography Quality Standards Act (MQSA). Our streamlined application process has cut approval time in half, with accreditation evaluation typically completed within 90 days of image submission.

Scheduling

All exams must be scheduled, with the exception of X-Rays(does not include IVP).

To schedule an appointment, please call 772 – 468 – 7020 Monday thru Friday 8am – 5pm Extended night and weekend times available

Important Appointment Information:

  • Cat Scan

    Cat Scan exams are performed at our Fort Pierce location only and take approximately 30 minutes

  • Ultrasound

    Ultrasound Ultrasound exams are performed at our Fort Pierce location and take approximately 20 minutes.

  • Mammography

    Mammography exams are also performed at our Fort Pierce location and take approximately 20 minutes.

  • Dexa Scans

    Dexa Scans are performed at our Fort Pierce location and take approximately 20 minutes.

  • X-ray

    X-ray exams are performed on a walk-in basis at our Fort Pierce location; except IVP exams which must be scheduled and take approximately 30 minutes.

  • Customer service provided by EaseUS

    We will provide top-rated customer service to your clients so that you're free from any trouble.

  • MRI/MRA

    MRI/MRA exams can be scheduled for either the Fort Pierce location or the St. Lucie West location. Depending on the specific exam schedule, appointment timescan range from 30 minutes to an hour and a half. Please plan accordingly.
